Homemade Chili

Back

Description

Homemade chili is delicious and goes great with cornbread or over corn chips for a chili pie! I like to use spicy pinto beans.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Canned goods
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Your chili will last for about four days if you store it properly.
  2. Transfer the cooled chili to an airtight container.
  3. Then store it in the refrigerator
  4. Reheat on the stove or in the microwave
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese on top and cook on Low until bubbly.
